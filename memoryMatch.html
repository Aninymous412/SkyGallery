Memory Match Game

Flip a card to start playing now!

Instructions

To play, click on a card to reveal its image. Click on another card to see if the images match. If they do, the cards will remain flipped. If the images don't match, the cards will turn back over. Keep flipping pairs of cards until you have discovered all the matches. If you get stuck, feel free to use the hints! Once you have matched all the cards, you win the game!
